Introduction

This is a super simple 720 panoramic viewer
  • You can use app to view the 720 degree panoramic photos or 720 degree panoramic video you took with panoramic camera.
  • The software provides a variety of projection modes, including "ball", "plane", "asteroid", "crystal ball", "panorama" or "360 degree panorama", to meet your viewing needs.
  • You can use the special VR glasses to open the panoramic VR mode for browsing, so you can get a better vr virtual reality browsing experience.
  • Support for panorama view when the screenshot, capture your current view of the panorama and save to the album.
  • You can import pictures or videos into app and support 1080p video decoding and playing.
  • The app has built-in classified favorite function, you can classify your multiple panoramas and quickly find them.
  • You can also select panorama in the mobile phone file manager, open it with other software, and open it with 720 panorama viewer to browse your panorama quickly
